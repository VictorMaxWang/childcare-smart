# Real User Scenarios

Updated: 2026-04-29

Browser Use threads must behave like ordinary users. Prefer navigation, visible buttons, cards, menus, and form controls over direct URL jumps.

## Login User

- Open the login page.
- Type a normal account and password.
- Toggle password visibility.
- Open and close the registration modal.
- Click each demo account entry: 陈园长, 李老师, 周老师, 林妈妈.
- Trigger login failure with invalid credentials and verify the error prompt.
- Check desktop and mobile layout for blocked inputs or buttons.

## Director: 陈园长

- Log in as 陈园长.
- Review home page data and cards.
- Enter the AI assistant.
- Enter weekly report and report views.
- Open child records.
- Open morning check and health views.
- Open diet records.
- Open growth records.
- Try filters, search, detail panels, drawers, and modals.
- Do not execute real delete, final submit, notification send, or irreversible confirmation.

## Teachers: 李老师 and 周老师

- Log in as 李老师 and 周老师 separately.
- Review the teacher workbench.
- Review class tasks.
- Open morning check.
- Open diet records.
- Open growth records.
- Open AI assistant and home-school communication.
- Open health material parsing.
- Open high-risk consultation.
- Try filters, search, draft, detail, modal, drawer, and tab interactions.
- Do not execute final submit, delete, upload confirmation, or irreversible send.

## Parent: 林妈妈

- Log in as 林妈妈.
- Review child home page.
- Review recent 7-day records and trends.
- Review feedback entry and feedback section.
- Review growth storybook.
- Browse on mobile viewport.
- Check feedback entry points and anchor behavior.
- Do not submit real feedback.
